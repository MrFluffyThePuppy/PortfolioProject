About

Hey there! I'm Evan McKane, and this is my first ever website that I have created/followed through the Treehouse program. I am 19 years old. I'm a passionate gamer who loves characters and coming up with stories and worlds for each character to live in. I want to be a video game story-writer because I feel that this will be the new pathway for storytelling heading into the future, and well, video games are a huge part of my life! I'm probably going to come back and change the portfolio portion and link the site to some pages of some characters I have. Or just type them all out for you guys to read! Cheers!

If you would like to follow me on twitter and/or add me on facebook, the icons will link you over to my accounts so you can follow and/or add me. Feel free to, not mandatory what-so-ever.
